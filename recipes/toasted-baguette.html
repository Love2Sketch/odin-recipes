Cheesy Toasted Baguette with Ham

Home

Description

Enjoy this as an easy warm snack or lunch!

Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Deli Ham (2 slices)
  • Provolone Cheese (2 slices)
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

Directions:

  1. Cut about about 4.5 inches of baguette and slice down the middle so that you have two halves.
  2. Butter both halves of baguette liberally.
  3. Fold 2 thin slices of ham in half and place one on each baguette peice.
  4. Cut or rip two slices of smoked provolone cheese in half and place two halves on each baguette covering the ham.
  5. Season with lots of garlic powder and Italian seasoning.
  6. Place on tinfoil and broil in the oven on Low for serveral minutes until the cheese is bubbling and browning.
  7. Enjoy your delicious toasted baguette!
