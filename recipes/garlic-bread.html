Classic Garlic Bread

Description

Crispy, toasty, classic garlic bread.

Ingredients:

  • French Bread
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Slice bread into about 1 inch thick slices and place on pizza pan covered in foil.
  3. Butter each face-up side.
  4. Season with garlic powder to taste.
  5. Add as much or as little cheese as you please.
  6. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
  7. Bake in the oven for about 8 minutes or until cheese is browning and the bread is toasted.
  8. Take out of the oven and enjoy!
