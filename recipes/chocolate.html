Description

This is a super easy and fun way to make homemade untempered dark chocolate with only a microwave.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup of Refined Coconut Oil
  • 1/2 Cup of Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 3/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
  • (Optional) Vanilla Extract
  • (Optional) Chrushed Peppermints

Directions:

  1. Place coconut oil in a bowl and microwave until fully melted.
  2. Mix in cocoa powder until fully incorperated.
  3. Slowly mix in powdered sugar. Mix well until smooth.
  4. If desired, add in 1/2 teaspoon or less vanilla extract. Stir.
  5. Pour chocolate into silicone molds or into a small shallow dish lined with parchment paper to make bark.
  6. If desired, add crushed peppermints on top of bark.
  7. Place chocolate into the freezer or refrigerator and let harden.
  8. Pop fully set chocolate out of molds or lift out of container. If you made bark, cut into smaller jagged pieces.
  9. Enjoy your chocolate!
